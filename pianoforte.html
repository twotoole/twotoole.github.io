PianoForte

Link to online playable version: twotoole.itch.io/pianoforte
Link to scripts: github.com/twotoole/PIANOFORTE-Scripts-

This was a game for a univeristy module. The controller is made from wood and the electonics for USB arcade buttons. You control microgames with the piano controller while corresponding piano key sounds play.

At the time I was going to a lot of game showcases and installations and wanted to make a custom controller and game experience. The idea is that you're playing as a concert pianist and you're daydreaming about other potential occupations.
