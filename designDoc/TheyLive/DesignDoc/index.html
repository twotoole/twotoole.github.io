    • The goal for this project is to create a game where the player feels like they are in control of Roddy Piper in the world of They Live.
    The current overview:

    • Stating 'hub' location of the street/kiosk where Piper first puts on the glasses.
      • the player will be unarmed
      • weapons are to be procured from enemies, in missions, and from exploration of open zone
      • can talk to any non-alien. they give some directional dialogue. Non-aliens wearing glasses give a mission.
      • After some amount of time eliminating exclusively aliens, someone in glasses will come up to you to give you a mission or tells you about a meet up.
    • isolated areas for discrete missions.
    • the game will end when the player has obtained a communication watch (used by aliens) and uses it.
