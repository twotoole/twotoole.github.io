Tomorrow Never Dies

Notes
  • Mission based
      • 1st Mission
    • Stealth but no vision cones
      • Enemies notice when a nearby enemy is dead
    • Auto lock on aiming, also first person aim option
    • trigger point in the mission when you complete the main objective, alarm sounds and reinforcments arrive, presumable everyone is hostile.
      • picked up access card (card to leave level) from the final reinforcement, so you have to beat every enemy (i.e. no stealth)
      2nd Mission
      • Objective is to take pictures of military arms
      • row of obstacles can be destroyed with an explosive
      • combat arena with countdown timer
        • leading to big turret sequence (pretty cool tbf)
      3rd Mission
    • objective is to rendezvous with NPC
    • setup is a party so your man has a suit on and start with emphasis on stealth
      • encounter behind enemy and can do stealth knockout
    • almost immediately it's all hostile enemies and combat rooms
    • you can kneel (presumably for cover reasons) and you can dodge roll while kneeling
    • there's a lot of shooting large machinery to destroy it.
    • different idle and shooting animation when at low health
    • a lot of opening doors and immediately shooting everyone inside
      4th Mission
    • Non hostile NPCS, completely neutral
    • set piece enemies, death cutscenes
    • has an escape the building ending, where everyone is hostile
      5th Mission
    • Start in suit, enemies will ignore you if you dont have a weapon out.
    • everyone in the level is an enemy that is can shoot (consierge, bartender)
      • You take out the entire room, and still have to talk to the one remaining bartender to get the next objective (kinda kills the wibe)
        • but if you talk to the barman fist you are forced to shoot, causing the entire room to be hostile regardless.
    • first bossfight - he has bare health
      6th Mission
    • It's night so yo've got infra-red goggles and are taking people out from a distance
    • Car combat mission
      7th Mission
    • skiing mission
    • more nightvision sniper stuff
    • you can shoot out windows

What was good/interesting?

  • there is a clear 2 item system where R1/2 select a weapon, and L1/2 select a contextual item (e.g. cufflinks, fingerprint, healthpack)
  • the setup for each mission seems intriguing and different. However the level generally plays out the same.

How can this influence They Live

  • Every item could be very important
    • you have to do something specific in a mission to get the goggles -> the goggles influence how you take on a future mission
