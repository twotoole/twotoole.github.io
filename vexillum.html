Vexillum

Link to scripts: github.com/twotoole/Vexillum

This game uses the flagcdn.com api to load the images of every included flag, a flag is then randomly selected and the colour of random points on the flag are polled to generate an enemy for the level, where each type of enemy has unique behaviour.
As you progress the number of enemies increases every 5 levels and the player health increases every 10 levels.

  • Made using the p5.js and p5.play libraries
